Heads-up: every price below is an estimate. Click to read why

Irish childcare chains do not publish their fees publicly. Giraffe, Cocoon, Little Harvard and Tigers all confirmed today (April 2026) that their websites show no monthly or weekly figures. The numbers on the cards below are estimates, sourced as follows:

  • Chain crèches (Giraffe, Cocoon, Little Harvard, Tigers): a chain-wide rate inherited from earlier research. Not pulled from the chain's website, because they don't publish.
  • Core-Funding providers: capped at €295/week by government rule, so we use that range as a fallback when the provider doesn't publish.
  • Sessional Montessori & playschools: figures reflect the ECCE capitation rate; cost to parents during ECCE hours is €0.
  • Childminders: typical Tusla-registered childminder rate, not published per minder.
  • Independents marked (est.): pure estimates within the Core-Funding cap range.
  • Waitlist length: sector-wide guess (40,000+ on national waitlists per SIPTU/Early Childhood Ireland), not provider-specific.

So: every fee on this page starts as ? Unconfirmed. When you ring or email a provider and they tell you the real number, click the ✎ next to their fee and save it. The card will switch to ✓ You verified and remember it next time you visit.

Subsidy guide

Every scheme, in plain English.

Four schemes interact. Use them in combination: that's how you get the bill down.

Universal · Free

ECCE: Early Childhood Care & Education

Two years of free preschool, 3 hours/day, 5 days/week, 38 weeks/year.

  • Eligibility: age 2y 8m – 5y 6m
  • Capitation to provider: €69–€82 / wk / child
  • Cost to parent: €0 (for the ECCE hours)
Universal · Not means-tested

NCS Universal

€2.14/hour off your bill, for every child aged 24 weeks to 15 years.

  • Max 45 hours/week
  • Worth up to €96.30/week per child
  • Stacks with ECCE for non-ECCE hours
Means-tested

NCS Income-Assessed

Bigger hourly subsidy based on household income.

  • Now: Full subsidy < €26k · graduated to €60k
  • Sept 2026: Full < €34k · up to €68k
  • New €11k deduction per additional child
Provider-side cap

Core Funding fee cap

Partner providers can't raise fees above government caps.

  • €295/wk max for full day (40–50 hrs)
  • €198.70/wk after Universal NCS
  • Government target: €200/month per child

About & more

Why this exists.

This site is for the mothers and fathers without a support system.

I'm a mother of a wobbler. I had to pay more than half of my salary in childcare, and finding a place near home was its own struggle. So I'm giving back what the system gave me, using the internet to make a free tool that any parent can use, copy, and adapt for their own part of Ireland.

Fork it, change the providers list, point it at your own town. The code is on GitHub.
